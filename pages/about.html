About me

Barbi is musician/bass player, dancer, songwriter and choreographer. Currently she is studying in EMC (Escuela de Música Conteporánea), plays as a bass player in a band with various styles (funk, soul, blues and r&b) and works as a choreographic coach in musical comedies. Join her and enjoy the ride!!!
